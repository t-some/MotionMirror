MotionMirror

Do you want to check your own motion (form) ?
With this app, you can record your own video at the timing you want to take by remote operation.
After the recording is finished, it will be played automatically, so you can check immediately on the spot.
Please use it to check and improve the form such as golf and hitting swing.

Features

  • Remote operations of video recording, playback and saving are possible with gestures by face and finger detections.
  • Your motion and recording start timing can be adjusted by the wait time after the remote recording instruction.
  • Four playback speeds.
  • Remote operation by smiling or closing eyes can also be selected.
